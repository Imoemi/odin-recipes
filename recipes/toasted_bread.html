Toasted Bread

Image of a toasted bread

Toasted bread is a top tier breakfast for any household. It's easy and quick to make and could even be packed to be eaten later.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Onions
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Salt, Pepper and seasoning cube for taste
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Break egg into a small bowl and add salt, knorr, onion and pepper
  2. Mix together properly
  3. Pour groundnut oil into frying pan and allow to heat up for like 10 seconds
  4. Add egg to groundnut oil and fry till its brown
  5. Slice bead into half and put egg in the middle
  6. Put bread in the frying pan and allow to fry for few seconds. Not too brown too
